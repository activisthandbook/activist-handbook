During this course for rebels, you will learn all about the basics of web development. You will work together with your fellow students in small groups to learn how to build your own web apps. Like all our courses, no previous experience is required to follow along.

This course is part of Rebel Academy, an initiative developed by Activist Handbook to stimulate peer-to-peer learning.
