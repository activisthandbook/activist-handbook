Responsibilities

Like all roles at Activist Handbook, the responsibilities that come with the position of project coordinator vary per person, depending on your personal experiences and preferences. We highly recommend you to discuss your responsibilities with your fellow coordinators and team members.

The responsibilities below serve as an indication, and may be delegated or change over time:

  • Keeping an overview of what various teams are doing
  • Facilitating coordinator meetings
  • Ensuring continuity of coordinators team
  • Ensuring tasks with priority are taken over if another coordinator becomes unavailable
  • Manage project priorities in close corporation with other coordinators
  • Keeping the workload of fellow coordinators in check
