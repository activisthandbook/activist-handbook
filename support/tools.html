In this article we explain what digital tools our volunteers use frequently. 

Need some help? Send a message to our tech support: contact@activisthandbook.org

Slack

We use Slack to communicate. Make sure to download the app and turn on notifications. Join our Slack using this invite link.

Google Drive

We save all our files in our Google Drive folder. Need editing access? Feel free to ask our tech support!

Discord

Every day between 14:00 and 16:00 UTC we host a virtual workspace. During these times, join our audio channels in Discord. Join our Discord using this invite link.
