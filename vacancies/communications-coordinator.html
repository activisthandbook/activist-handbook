Activist Handbook's international community of change-makers is growing fast. We are looking for a Communications Coordinator. Will you join our coordination team?

Our Communications Coordinator is responsible for managing all our online communication and leading the communications team. You will be recruiting new members, come up with a social media strategy, and send out our monthly newsletter. Together with the rest of the coordination team you will be building a collaborative manual for activists all over the world.

Interested?

Send a short motivation letter (max 1 page) and CV to contact@activisthandbook.org. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions regarding this position.

What will you be doing as Communications Coordinator?

  • You lead our communications team.
  • You manage the social media accounts such as Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.
  • You produce good looking content: illustrations, videos, gifs and more
  • You manage our online advertising budget.
  • You write our monthly newsletter.
  • You closely cooperate with your fellow coordinators.

Who are we looking for?

  • You wholeheartedly agree with our principles.
  • You have experience as an activist, are politically involved or have shown interest in changing our society for the better in other ways.
  • You have experience with managing social media.
  • You have experience with designing illustrations and editing videos.
  • You like working in a team. You understand leading is not about telling people what to do but enabling them to come to their full potential.
  • You are able to write English flawlessly.

What are we offering?

  • You will get to grow your network and make lots of new international friends.
  • You will work together with a close team of enthusiastic and motivated volunteers who are not afraid to think big.
  • You will be able to learn everything about effective change-making from experienced activists from all over the world.
  • We are currently in the process of officially registering our organisation and finding funds. Part of these funds we will be using to pay a small allowance to our volunteers.
