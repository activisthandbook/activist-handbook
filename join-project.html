Activist Handbook is a Wikipedia-like platform for organisations and individuals to share and find knowledge on activism - for activists, by activists. Our core team exists out of about 10 people and more than 60 people have joined our Facebook group.

We are inviting you to join the project! Fill in our form to become involved and stay updated. After filling in the form you'll receive an invite link for our next open video call for new members.

We meet online

We're activists from all over the world, so we cannot regularly meet face to face. Instead, we regularly have video calls. Are you new with Activist Handbook? We would very much like to get to know you during our next call! We also have an open Facebook group in which activists can exchange experiences. Lastly, we communicate using a Slack workplace. If you would like to join our Slack, make sure to fill in the form above.

We value diversity

We believe that diversity strengthens our organisation. That is why we encourage people of all genders, sexual preferences and ethnic backgrounds, including people with disabilities, to join our cause.
