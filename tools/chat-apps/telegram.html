Telegram is an open source messaging app. It's a chat app, but it also allows for massive communities. Some of the largest communities out there. 75k people per group. 

TL;DR: Telegram offers great features for large movements. However, it one of the least secure and privacy friendly options out there. Also make sure to check out our reviews of other chats apps.

 



Public channels are not end to end encrypted. 

Private messages can be end to end encrypted though. 

Telegram is useful if you want to keep a super large number of people informed on a subject in a semi public way with the option for secure private messaging.
