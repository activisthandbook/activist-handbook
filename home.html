Our community

  • Resources: All articles on Activist Handbook have a resource section at the bottom. In addition, we have a large list of resources in various languages about activism in general.
  • Activist communities: Progressive activist organisations can create their own section on this website where they can add manuals. Instead of having Google Docs all over the place, we build a platform specifically for activists to work on articles together.
