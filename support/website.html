Permission groups 🔐

  • 👑 Administrators (highest level): Admins can do anything.
  • ⭐️ Moderators: Moderators can do anything content related, including deleting pages and managing comments. They cannot manage permission groups and users. They cannot embed scripts to the website, manage the theme or api.
  • 🌏Translators: Translators have the same permissions as editors, expect that they can also manage the left-side navigation menu so that they can translate it.
  •  📝 Editors: Anyone who creates an account becomes an editor. Editors can edit pages. They cannot delete pages or upload files.
  • 👻 Guests (lowest level): Guests can read pages.
