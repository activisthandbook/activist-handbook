Activist Handbook is written by activists like you. We are building this knowledge base together. You can start contributing too by creating an account!

  • Write an article: Any contribution is valued, no matter how small. Create a completely new page, add some resources to a page, or correct a typo. Make sure to read our guidelines on how to write good articles!
  • Translate a page: We want to make Activist Handbook accessible for everyone. That is why we have a team of translators who manage the localised versions of our platform.
  • Moderate contributions: Every edit is saved forever. This makes it easier for us to moderate. You can view version history of pages on our website, as well as via our backup at GitHub. If you see any vandalism, do not hesitate to contact us or to fix it yourself.
  • Become a developer: Activist Handbook is an open source project. We very much welcome you to contribute. We made a page for developers where we document how our platform was built.

If you would like to become more involved, you can join our volunteer team! Anyone who is interested is welcome to become a volunteer. Learn more about how to join us.
