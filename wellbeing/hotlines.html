This is a list of sites and hotlines where you can get help. Please do not hesitate to reach out! 

Wherever you are, there are people that are there to help you. You can find a good overview of resources on https://checkpointorg.com/global/. 

If you are a teen have a look at https://www.childhelplineinternational.org/child-helplines/child-helpline-network/ to find local resources for your country. 

If you are experiencing thoughts of harming yourself please refer to http://www.suicide.org/international-suicide-hotlines.html for a phone hotline for your country.
