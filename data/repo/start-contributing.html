How to write a good article

Here are some tips to write good articles:

  • Texts should be simple to read. Many visitors of this site are non-native English speakers. Short sentences are better than long ones, and simple words are better than difficult ones.
  • Reference to resources that already exist on a topic. Do not try to reinvent the wheel, it is very likely that someone else has already done some great work.
  • Make sure to give credit to those who deserve it. Are you adapting work from other sources which was published under a creative commons licence? Add the name of the author you based your work on. And, if possible, add a link to the original work as well.
  • Your article does not have to be perfect. Others are here to help you.
  • Use titles to structure your article. Use short instead of long titles.
