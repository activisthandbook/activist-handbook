This design guide is intended as a quick reference, for example when creating illustrations for our social media. Like all content on this website, this design guide is available under a creative commons licence. We do ask, however, only to use our design guide if you adhere to our principles.

Colours

We use a bright and bold color scheme to reflect our culture of change-making. 

We want to make sure our design identity looks consistent across platforms. Take special care when printing or when publishing to online platforms where strong compression is applied to images.

  • Red: #FF006C
  • Dark red: #e0005e (only use this color to make text more readable)
  • Blue: #062CFF
  • Black: #000000

Fonts

We chose to use Noto Sans as it fits well with our international orientation. Noto Sans was specifically designed to look consistent across different character sets. The font is available for almost 100 different languages.

On our website we don't use our custom fonts in order to enhance page load speed. In our graphics on social media, however, we do use these fonts. 

  • Main text: Noto Sans (Regular/Bold/Italics)
  • Large headings Noto Sans Display (Condensed Black)
