Are you bilingual? Help us make Activist Handbook available in your language! Here's how you can help:

  • Create an account and start translating articles today: Just click the globe button on the top of the page to switch languages. If a page in your language does not yet exist, feel free to create a new one.
  • Join our team of translation volunteers. Translating is more fun if you do it together! We highly recommend to join our team if you would like to contribute by translating.
  • Help us translate WikiJS: Our website was built using WikiJS, which is currently available in over 40 languages. You can help by translating user interface items (buttons, settings pages, etc.) More than 250 people have already helped to translate WikiJS!
