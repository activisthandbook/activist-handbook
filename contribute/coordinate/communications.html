In this guide, we will help you to get started as a communications coordinator. We encourage you to to keep this document up-to-date. This will help you and your successor! Also make sure to check out the other guides for coordinators.

Responsibilities

Like all roles at Activist Handbook, the responsibilities that come with the position of communication coordinator vary per person, depending on your personal experiences and preferences. We highly recommend you to discuss your responsibilities with your fellow coordinators and team members.

The responsibilities below serve as an indication, and may be delegated or change over time:

  • Facilitate communication team meetings
  • Set up a communications strategy
  • Present progress updates of the communications team
  • Write guides for the communications team 
  • Manage our social media
  • Welcome new members
