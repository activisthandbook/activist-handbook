In this guide, we explain when and how we meet. We highly recommend volunteers to subscribe to our Google Calendar.

Schedule

We follow a 4-week schedule, as outlined below. We encourage all volunteers to join our General and Governance meetings. 

Circles other than the core circle may schedule their own meetings as well. Check the relevant Slack channels for the dates of those meetings.

Virtual workspace

Every day between 14:00 and 16:00 UTC we host a virtual workspace using a Discord audio channel. Our virtual workspace is open for all volunteers to join whenever they want to work on Activist Handbook.

You can join our Discord using this invite link. Note that we first have to verify you before you can join any audio channel.

Meeting formats

Our meetings always follow a structured format. There are two formats that we use:

  • Tactical: General and circle meetings follow the tactical format of Holacracy.
  • Governance: Governance meetings follow the governance format of Holacracy.
