In this article we discuss various tools that you can use to broadcast text messages to your followers. Also make sure to check out our guide on chat apps to communicate internally in your activist movement.

Broadcasting

  • Twillio: Develop your own custom solution for sending text messages. Twillio supports SMS and WhatsApp.

Omnichannel dashboards

  • Eazy: Provides an easy to use dashboard where you can see all messages that you receive over multiple platforms.
