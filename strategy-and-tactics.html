Strategy provides the basis for everything that an organisation does, while tactics are the varying tools used by the organisation to realise its strategy. Read this section to understand more about both of these.

You can contribute: This handbook is written by people like you. Feel free to add tactics you know! Use this tactic page template if you are creating a new page. Find out more about how to contribute.
