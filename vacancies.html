What is Activist Handbook?

Activist Handbook is a Wikipedia-like site for everyone to exchange knowledge and experiences on activism. It is build by people like you. We recognise the urgency of solving the climate crisis and bringing the growing wealth gap to a halt. Together we want to make change-making more effective, and we believe that we can only achieve this by empowering the powerless. We welcome you to join our team! Check out our current team of volunteers on our about page.

If you would like to become involved with the Activist Handbook project, but are not ready to take up a leading role, check out our page on how to join.

Process of applying

  1. Let us know you are interested in a position.
  2. We will reach out to you and schedule a brief call. Here we will make sure to find a right place for you within our organisation.
  3. You start working together in a team, and we will gradually help you get started.
