Moderators uphold quality by proofreading, adding references and fixing vandalism. If you would like to help us uphold the quality of our content, make sure to join our team.

Ways in which you moderate:

  • Proofread new articles that our content team has written
  • Report vandalism to us or fix it yourself by using the version history.
  • Check recently edited pages on our dashboard and review the changes that were made. You will need to request the necessary permissions first.

Transparency

In principle, we allow anyone to contribute to our shared knowledge base on activism. However, everyone who wishes to do so, must follow our principles.

Any changes made are saved forever. We do this to provide transparency about who has contributed to Activist Handbook.
