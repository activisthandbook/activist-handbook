This is a list of sites and hotlines where you can get help. Please do not hesitate to reach out! 

  • List of emergency hotlines worldwide.
  • Wherever you are, there are people that are there to help you. You can find a good overview of resources on Check Point. 
  • If you are a teen have a look at Child Line International to find local resources for your country. 
  • If you are experiencing thoughts of harming yourself please refer to one of the international suicide hotlines for a phone hotline for your country.
