Why do we keep a logbook?

All Activist Handbook circles keep a logbook. We do this because:

  • We work more effectively by remembering our previous actions.
  • We want to be transparent about our decision making process.
  • We want to make it easier for new members to join.
  • We want to help fellow activists understand how to manage an organisation.

What do we record?

The following will be recorded by teams in our logbook:

  • Someone starts or stops as coordinator
  • Meetings, with minutes and recordings attached
  • Important decisions and actions
